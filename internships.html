Internships

Serious students can inquire about our remote business-analyst and programming internships which we offer from time to time.

Analyst interns are offered the opportunity to assist in evaluating companies and making investment decisions. After being assigned a group of stocks, each intern will be responsible for putting together presentations explaining company segments, key products, important movements in the stock price, pertinent balance sheet information, earnings estimates, etc. and coming up with an investment recommendation.

Programming interns are given the opportunity to acquire hand-on expertise with a variety of languages and tools.

To apply download and complete the internship questionnaire and email your answers along with your resume and cover letter to elleL@live.com
